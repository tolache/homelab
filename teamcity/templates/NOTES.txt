To connect to TeamCity locally via LoadBalancer:
  open https://tc.{{ .Values.teamcity.domainName }}
or
  open http://{{ .Values.teamcity.lanHostname }}

To connect to TeamCity via ClusterIP:
  kubectl -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} port-forward svc/teamcity 8111:8111
  open http://localhost:8111

The Postgres credentials are stored in Secret "{{ .Values.postgres.secretName }}".
